Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries? +
We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during working days (Monday to Friday, 8am-6pm).
Do you provide free quotes? +
Yes! We offer free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. Contact us to schedule an assessment on a working day.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve all 47 counties in Kenya including Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, Kajiado, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and all other counties.
Are repairs available on weekends? +
Repairs and consultations are available on working days only (Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM). For emergencies during working hours, please call our office.
What Mabati options do you offer? +
We offer all major Kenyan mabati profiles including Dumu zas (26, 28 gauge), Versatile (26, 28 gauge), Box Profile (0.3mm, 0.5mm), Roman Tiles, and Corrugated GCI sheets.
