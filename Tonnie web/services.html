Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services

What Mabati options do you offer? +
We offer a wide range of Kenyan roofing materials including Dumu zas (26, 28, 30 gauge), Versatile, Box Profile, Roman Tiles, Corrugated GCI sheets, and various gauges to suit your needs and budget.
Do you offer free quotes? +
Yes! We provide free, no-obligation quotes for all our services. Contact us to schedule an assessment on a working day.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve all 47 counties in Kenya including Nairobi, Kiambu, Machakos, Kajiado, Mombasa, Kisumu, Nakuru, and surrounding areas.
Are repairs available on weekends? +
Repairs and consultations are available on working days only (Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM). For urgent inquiries during working hours, please call our office.
