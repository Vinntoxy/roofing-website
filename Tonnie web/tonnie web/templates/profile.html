About Tonnie Roofing

Building trust through quality craftsmanship since 2015

Founder & CEO

John Tonnie

Visionary Leader in Construction

With over 20 years of experience in the construction industry, John Tonnie founded Tonnie Roofing in 2015 with a vision to provide quality roofing and interior design services to Kenyan homeowners and businesses. His hands-on approach and commitment to excellence has built a company known for reliability, quality craftsmanship, and customer satisfaction.

"I believe that every project, big or small, deserves the same level of attention and quality. Our team doesn't just build roofs; we build relationships and trust with our clients that last for generations."

John Tonnie

Our Journey

8 years of growth and excellence

2015

Company Founded

Tonnie Roofing was established in Nairobi with a vision to provide quality roofing services to Kenyan homeowners.

2017

Expansion to Interior Design

Added interior design services including gypsum installations and custom fittings to meet growing client demands.

2019

100th Project Completed

Celebrated a major milestone with our 100th successful project across Nairobi and surrounding counties.

2021

Introduction of Mabati Options

Expanded roofing materials to include all major Kenyan mabati profiles including Dumu zas, Versatile, and Box Profile.

2023

Expanded to 47 Counties

Extended our services to cover all 47 counties across Kenya, bringing quality roofing to every corner of the nation.

2026

250+ Projects & Growing

Continuing to serve our clients with dedication, quality, and professionalism across Kenya.
